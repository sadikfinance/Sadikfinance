SADIK FINANCE
Professional Prop Firm Trading Blueprint

Risk Management Framework

Never risk more than 0.5% to 1% per trade. Maintain a minimum 1:2 risk reward ratio. Daily loss limit should remain under 2% to protect account longevity.

Market Structure Strategy

Focus on liquidity sweeps, market structure shifts, fair value gaps and institutional order flow concepts. Trade only at premium or discount zones based on higher timeframe bias.

Entry Model

Use lower timeframe confirmation such as break of structure, displacement, mitigation blocks and confirmation candles before entering trades.

Psychology Control

Avoid revenge trading. Avoid increasing risk after wins. Emotional control is more important than strategy.

Challenge Phase Plan

Do not rush profit targets. One high probability trade per day is enough. Consistency beats frequency.

Scaling Strategy

Scale only after stable win rate and drawdown control. Do not increase lot size emotionally.

Journaling & Performance Tracking

Track win rate, average R:R, session performance, emotional state and mistakes. Improve based on data, not emotions.

Common Reasons Traders Fail

  • Overtrading
  • Breaking rules
  • Poor psychology
  • Inconsistent risk management
  • Switching strategies frequently
