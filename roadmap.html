📅 Week by Week Challenge Plan
Week 1 — Slow Start
Target: 2–3%
Trade minimum lot size. Risk only 0.5% per trade. Your only job this week is to NOT lose money and get comfortable with the platform. Take 2–3 high quality trades maximum. Do not chase profits. If you hit 3% this week you are perfectly on track — stop and wait for week 2.
Week 2 — Build Momentum
Target: 5–6%
Continue same approach. Look for 2–3 A+ setups only. By end of week 2 you should be at 5–6% total. If drawdown hits 3% in any single day this week — stop immediately and review. Never chase to recover weekly losses.
Week 3 — Target Zone
Target: 8%
You should be approaching the profit target. If already at 7–8% — reduce risk to 0.25% per trade. Do not take unnecessary risks to hit the exact target. Being 1% below target and safe is better than going over target and failing from a bad trade.
Week 4 — Protect & Close
Target: Pass ✅
This week is about protecting what you built. If target is hit — take 1 small trade per day maximum just to meet minimum trading day requirements. If target is not hit yet — take only the absolute best setups. One bad week 4 trade can erase an entire month of work.

⚠️ Why People Fail Challenges
Understanding why others fail is as important as knowing what to do. These are the real reasons 80% of challenges fail.
❌ #1 — Overtrading on Day 1
Traders open 5–10 trades on day 1 excited to start. They hit the daily loss limit immediately and the panic begins. Rule: Maximum 2 trades on day 1 of any challenge.
❌ #2 — Revenge Trading After First Loss
One loss leads to an emotional trade to recover. That second trade is always bigger. That second trade usually loses too. Walk away after any loss and come back tomorrow.
❌ #3 — Trading NFP/CPI Without Closing Positions
High impact news can move 100–200 pips in seconds. Holding a trade through NFP on a prop account is gambling. Close all trades 30 minutes before major news events.
❌ #4 — Rushing Profit Target in Week 3–4
Traders see they need 3% more with 5 days left and start gambling. They increase lot size and blow the account. The target will come naturally if you trade your plan consistently.
❌ #5 — Holding Trades Over Weekend
Markets gap on Sunday open. A 50 pip gap can hit your stop loss instantly or worse — blow through it. Never hold positions into the weekend on funded accounts. Always flat by Friday 3PM GMT.
